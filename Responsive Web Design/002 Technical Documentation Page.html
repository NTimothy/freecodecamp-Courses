Introduction

This page references step-by-step instructions provided in the: iHealth COVID-19 Antigen Rapid At-Home Test kit [Model ICO-3000/ICO-30001/ICO-30002]. By following the instructions on the page, it is possible to obtain results in approximately 15 minutes. It is important to note that, while this particular model has neither been cleared nor approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), it has been authorized under an Emergency Use Authorization.

Prepare Materials

TEST SET #1:

  • 1 x COVID-19 Test Card in Pouch
  • 1 x Pre-filled Tube
  • 1 x Swab

Open the package, take out the COVID-19 Test Card in the pouch, the Tube prefilled with the extraction solution, and the Swab. Since the tube has already been pre-filled for your convenience, you may proceed to the next step.

TEST SET #2:

  • 1 x COVID-19 Test Card in Pouch
  • 1 x Empty Tube
  • 1 x Sealed Solution
  • 1 x Swab
